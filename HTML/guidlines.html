Land Purchase Guidelines

Before buying land through our platform, please review these important terms and ethical guidelines to ensure a secure and responsible transaction.

1. Verify Legal Documents

Ensure that the land has a clear and updated title. All documents including deed, khatian, and mutation records should be authentic and verified.

2. Physical Site Visit

Physically visit the land location. Cross-check the plot boundaries, local environment, and infrastructure access (roads, utilities, etc.).

3. Fair Dealings

Conduct negotiations transparently. Avoid exploiting sellers in financial distress and maintain honesty throughout the deal.

4. Ethical Compliance

Buy land with respect to environmental laws, land zoning regulations, and without displacing vulnerable communities unfairly.

5. Check Land Tax Status

Make sure the land taxes are paid up to date. Outstanding tax issues may cause legal troubles for new owners.

6. Secure Personal Data

Share your identity and contact information only through trusted agents or directly via our secure platform.
